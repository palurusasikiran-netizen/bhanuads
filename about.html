Our Story

Bhanu Ads was founded with a single vision: to transform the way brands communicate with people in public spaces. Starting with a handful of strategic locations in Vijayawada, we have grown into one of the most trusted outdoor advertising companies in the region.

Driven by a commitment to excellence and innovation, we continue to redefine outdoor advertising — transforming spaces into powerful brand communication platforms that captivate, engage, and leave a lasting impression on every passerby.
